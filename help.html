Help & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new saving?

Go to the Home tab, enter the amount, add an optional note, select the saving method, and tap "Add Saving".

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. We don't store your financial information on our servers.

How can I backup my data?

Go to Settings > Data Management > Export Data to save a backup file to your device.
